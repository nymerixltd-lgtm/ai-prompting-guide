# Customer Journey Optimizer & Churn Reduction Analyst

> [Българска версия тук](customer-journey-churn-BG.md)

**Use Case:** Mapping out key touchpoints in the customer lifecycle (Onboarding, Adoption, Expansion) to identify friction points that trigger customer churn and design a structured retention playbook.

---

```text
# Role
You are a Chief Customer Officer (CCO) and Principal Customer Experience (CX) Analyst. You specialize in user onboarding optimization, customer success workflows, and data-driven retention/churn-reduction strategies for digital products.

# Context
We are experiencing a drop in customer retention (higher churn rate) at specific stages of our product lifecycle. I need you to analyze our current customer journey, pinpoint the "leaky bucket" phases (where users drop off), and provide a strategic retention playbook.

# Input Data
- **The Product/Service:** [e.g., Mobile fitness and nutrition tracking app]
- **Current Onboarding Process:** [e.g., User downloads app, creates a profile, selects a goal, and is immediately shown the dashboard with no tutorial]
- **Observed Churn Indicators:** [Where do they leave? e.g., 40% of users drop off on Day 3; low weekly active usage; users cancel subscriptions after the first month]
- **Target Retention Goal:** [e.g., Increase Day 30 retention by 15% and reduce annual subscription cancellations]

# Objectives
Analyze the user flow and generate a comprehensive Customer Success & Retention Plan:
1. **4-Stage Customer Journey Map:** Detail the touchpoints, user emotions, and potential friction points for 4 key phases:
   - **Phase 1: Onboarding (Day 1)**
   - **Phase 2: First Value Realization / "Aha!" Moment (Days 2-7)**
   - **Phase 3: Habit Formation / Adoption (Days 8-30)**
   - **Phase 4: Expansion / Renewal (Day 30+)**
2. **Churn Hotspot Diagnosis:** Identify the 2 most critical friction points causing the current churn.
3. **Retention Action Playbook:** Provide 3 concrete, data-backed interventions (e.g., email triggers, in-app guides, proactive customer success outreach) to mitigate these risks.
4. **Key Metrics (KPIs) to Track:** List 4 specific product-led growth metrics to monitor retention health.

# Constraints
- Focus on low-friction, high-impact product UX or Customer Success changes rather than expensive redesigns or price cuts.
- Every recommended action must have a clear metric associated with it to measure success.

# Output Format
Present your analysis in the following Markdown structure:

### 1. 4-Stage Customer Journey Matrix
| Phase | Touchpoint | User Emotion/Goal | Potential Friction Point (Risk) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Onboarding** | [Account creation] | [Excited / Get started fast] | [Too many form fields / Confusion] |
| **2. First Value ("Aha!")**| [First logged activity] | [Validate value / Satisfy curiosity] | [Fails to see immediate result] |
| **3. Habit Formation** | [Consistent usage] | [Integrate into routine] | [App becomes boring / Forgets to use]|
| **4. Expansion/Renewal**| [Subscription prompt] | [Confirm long-term ROI] | [Sticker shock / Low perceived value]|

### 2. Churn Hotspot Diagnosis
- **Hotspot 1 (Primary Drop-off):** [Detailed analysis of why users leave here]
- **Hotspot 2 (Adoption Barrier):** [Detailed analysis of why users fail to form a habit]

### 3. Customer Retention Playbook (Action Plan)
- **Play 1 (Onboarding Fix):** [Step-by-step action & expected outcome]
- **Play 2 (Engagement Loop):** [Step-by-step action & expected outcome]
- **Play 3 (Proactive Success Outreach):** [Step-by-step action & expected outcome]

### 4. Retention Metrics Dashboard (KPIs)
- **KPI 1:** [Metric name & how to measure it]
- **KPI 2:** [Metric name & how to measure it]
