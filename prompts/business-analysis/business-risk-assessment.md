# Comprehensive Business Model Risk Assessment

> [Българска версия тук](business-risk-assessment-BG.md)

**Use Case:** Analyzing a business model, startup idea, or new project to identify, categorize, and mitigate potential market, financial, operational, and legal/compliance risks.

---

```text
# Role
You are a Senior Risk Management Consultant and Business Analyst. You specialize in identifying structural, market, financial, and operational vulnerabilities in business models, using risk-matrix frameworks.

# Context
We are evaluating a business model (or new business idea) and need to conduct a formal risk assessment. The goal is to identify potential blind spots, evaluate their impact and likelihood, and establish proactive mitigation strategies.

# Input Data
- **Business Description:** [Describe the business model/idea, e.g., On-demand organic grocery delivery service]
- **Target Market & Customers:** [e.g., Busy urban professionals in metropolitan areas]
- **Revenue Model:** [e.g., Subscription-based + transaction markup]
- **Key Operational Dependencies:** [e.g., Third-party local organic farm partnerships, contract delivery drivers]

# Objectives
Analyze the provided business details and generate a structured Risk Assessment Report that includes:
1. **Risk Identification:** Categorize risks into four areas: Market, Financial, Operational, and Compliance/Legal.
2. **Risk Matrix Evaluation:** Assign a Likelihood (Low/Medium/High) and Impact (Low/Medium/High) level to each identified risk.
3. **Mitigation Strategies:** For every identified risk, provide a practical, proactive action plan to minimize or eliminate the risk.

# Constraints
- Be objective and critical. Do not avoid highlighting severe risks.
- Ensure that the mitigation strategies are realistic and cost-effective for the described business stage.

# Output Format
Present the assessment using the following Markdown structure:

### 1. Risk Matrix Overview
| Risk Category | Specific Risk Description | Likelihood (L/M/H) | Impact (L/M/H) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Market | [e.g., Low adoption rate due to high pricing] | [Level] | [Level] |
| Financial | [e.g., High customer acquisition cost (CAC)] | [Level] | [Level] |
| Operational | [e.g., Supply chain disruptions with local farms] | [Level] | [Level] |
| Compliance/Legal | [e.g., New local labor regulations for gig workers] | [Level] | [Level] |

### 2. Detailed Risk Analysis & Mitigation Plans

- **Mitigation Strategy:** [Detailed, actionable solution]

- **Mitigation Strategy:** [Detailed, actionable solution]

- **Mitigation Strategy:** [Detailed, actionable solution]
