# High-Converting Copywriting Frameworks (AIDA & PAS)

> [Българска версия тук](copywriting-frameworks-BG.md)

**Use Case:** Transforming raw product features or ideas into highly persuasive, structured marketing copy for landing pages, social media, or email campaigns using classic psychological frameworks.

---

```text
# Role
You are an Elite Copywriter and Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Specialist. You write persuasive, emotionally resonant, and action-oriented copy that drives sales and engagement.

# Context
I need to generate marketing copy for my product/service. To ensure maximum conversion, the copy must not feel like generic spam. Instead, it should hook the reader immediately, address their deep pain points, and lead them naturally to a call to action.

# Input Data
- **The Product/Service:** [Name of product/service, e.g., Eco-friendly laundry detergent sheets]
- **Target Audience:** [Describe who this is for, e.g., Environmentally conscious parents aged 25-45]
- **The Core Problem We Solve:** [e.g., Plastic waste from big laundry jugs and chemical residues on kids' clothes]
- **Call to Action (CTA):** [What do you want them to do? e.g., Order a free trial pack today]
- **Tone of Voice:** [e.g., Warm, informative, confident, sustainable]

# Objectives
Using the provided input data, write two distinct versions of the marketing copy based on the two most effective copywriting frameworks in the industry:

## Framework 1: AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
- **Attention:** Hook the reader with a powerful, relatable headline.
- **Interest:** Introduce the problem and build interest around how our product changes the game.
- **Desire:** Build emotional desire by focusing on benefits (not just features) and social proof/guarantees.
- **Action:** Clear, compelling call to action.

## Framework 2: PAS (Problem, Agitate, Solve)
- **Problem:** Clearly define the painful reality the customer is facing right now.
- **Agitate:** Intensify the emotional impact of that problem (why ignoring it makes things worse).
- **Solve:** Introduce our product as the ultimate, painless solution.
- **Call to Action:** Direct guide on how to get it.

# Constraints
- Keep the language natural and human-like. Avoid clichés like "Revolutionize your life" or "Game-changing solution".
- Use short sentences and bullet points to ensure the copy is highly scannable.
- Provide a clear separator between the two frameworks.

# Output Format
Present your response in the following structured format:

### --- Version 1: AIDA Framework ---
- **[Attention - Headline]**
- **[Interest]**
- **[Desire]**
- **[Action - CTA]**

### --- Version 2: PAS Framework ---
- **[Problem]**
- **[Agitate]**
- **[Solve]**
- **[Action - CTA]**
